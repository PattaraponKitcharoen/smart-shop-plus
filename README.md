# 🛒 Smart Shop Plus
**A Smart Shopping List App with Price Tracking & Aisle Organization**

Smart Shop Plus is a mobile application built with **React Native** designed to help users shop more efficiently. It doesn't just list items; it tracks historical prices, compares current costs, and organizes your shopping journey by store and aisle.

## ✨ Key Features
*   🏬 **Multi-Store Support**: Organize your lists based on the specific supermarket or store you are visiting.
*   📍 **Aisle-Based Organization**: Group items by aisle or category to minimize walking time and avoid backtracking.
*   💰 **Smart Price Tracking**: Compare the **Last Price** paid with the **Current Price**. The app automatically calculates price differences and highlights savings or price hikes.
*   🛒 **Real-time Cart Summary**: A dedicated **Cart screen** that summarizes your total expenditure and price fluctuations before you reach the checkout.
*   🚀 **Smooth Animations**: Uses `LayoutAnimation` for a fluid and native feel when managing categories.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
*   **Frontend**: React Native (Expo)
*   **State Management**: Zustand
*   **Database**: Expo SQLite (Local storage for offline capability)
*   **Icons**: FontAwesome 5
